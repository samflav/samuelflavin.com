Pyramids

Expected Time
  • 10 min warmup
  • 40 min workout
  • 10 min cooldown
Instructions

Location: Gym Annex, adjacent to the CrossFit area.
Perform as many rounds of the pyramid as possible within 40 minutes.

Warm Up

  • Arm Swings × 15
  • Reverse Lunges × 15
  • High Knees – 15 seconds
  • Arm Rotations × 10
  • Run 1 lap

Pyramid

  • 5 Burpees
  • 10 Lunges
  • 15 Mountain Climbers
  • 20 Push-ups
  • 25 Jumping Jacks
  • 30-Second Plank
  • 25 Russian Twists
  • 20 Shoulder Taps
  • 15 Mountain Climbers
  • 10 Squats
  • 5 Burpees
  • Rest – 45 seconds

Cool Down

  • 1 Lap (slow)
  • Triceps Stretch
  • Shoulder Stretch
  • Chest Stretch
  • Quad Stretch
  • Calf Stretch
  • Pigeon Pose
  • Hamstring Stretch
  • Butterfly
