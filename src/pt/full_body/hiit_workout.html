HIIT Workout

Expected Time
  • 5 min warmup
  • 45 min workout
  • 5 min cooldown
Instructions

Location: Gym Annex, adjacent to the CrossFit area.

Warm Up

  • Jumping jacks × 25
  • Arm rotations × 10
  • 2 laps (fast walk / slow jog)

Round 1 – 30s Work / 15s Rest

  • Mountain climbers
  • Russian twist
  • High knees
  • Calf raises
  • Air squats

Interim: 2 laps run

Rest

Round 2

  • Jumping jacks
  • Lunges
  • Push-ups
  • Low plank
  • Shoulder taps / High plank to Low plank

Interim: 2 laps run

Rest

Cool Down

  • Triceps Stretch
  • Shoulder Stretch
  • Chest Stretch
  • Quad Stretch
  • Calf Stretch
  • Pigeon Pose
  • Hamstring Stretch
  • Butterfly
